Core areas of support

Support is tailored to the stage, scale, and complexity of the initiative, from early strategic definition to implementation planning and stakeholder engagement.

Strategy & Transformation

For organizations navigating growth, change, or emerging priorities that need clearer direction and a practical path forward.

This work may include strategic planning support, initiative design, organizational assessments, executive briefings, decision frameworks, and translation of broad priorities into focused action.

Digital Health & AI Readiness

For organizations trying to understand what digital transformation, AI, data, or emerging technology means for their workforce, members, governance, programs, or strategy.

This work may include AI and digital health education, readiness assessments, stakeholder discussions, strategy briefs, practical frameworks, and support translating emerging technology into responsible organizational action.

Stakeholder Alignment & Execution

For initiatives that require cross-functional coordination, executive communication, and sustained movement from discussion to delivery.

This work may include stakeholder mapping, meeting design, working group support, leadership communications, implementation planning, project coordination, and the materials needed to move work forward.

Grants & Program Development

For organizations developing new programs, funding concepts, strategic partnerships, or grant opportunities that require structure, narrative, and execution support.

This work may include opportunity assessment, concept development, proposal strategy, partner coordination, program design, first-draft development, review support, and translation of complex ideas into fundable and actionable plans.

How this can show up

Galanou Consulting can support focused projects, fractional strategic capacity, or defined workstreams that help an organization move from broad intent to usable outputs.

  • Strategic briefs and recommendations
  • Board or executive communication materials
  • Stakeholder convenings and discussion guides
  • AI or digital health education resources
  • Member-facing tools, frameworks, or reports
  • Program plans, roadmaps, and implementation support
  • Grant concepts, proposal narratives, and partner materials
  • Working group design, facilitation support, and synthesis

Best fit

Galanou Consulting is a strong fit when your organization:

  • Has an important priority that is still too broad or undefined
  • Needs to align internal and external stakeholders
  • Is navigating AI, digital health, workforce, member value, or organizational change
  • Needs focused strategic support without adding a full-time role
  • Has promising ideas, but needs help turning them into structured programs, proposals, or deliverables
  • Wants practical outputs, not just discussion

Does this sound like your organization?

Many organizations do not need a large consulting engagement. They need a focused partner who can help clarify the problem, organize the work, and move the next phase forward.
