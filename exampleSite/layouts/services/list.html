Engagement formats

Structured to match the priority.

Engagements can be structured as focused projects, defined workstreams, or fractional strategic capacity.

Focused projects

For a specific strategic question, deliverable, grant concept, briefing, or initiative that needs definition and movement.

Defined workstreams

For multi-step work that requires stakeholder input, synthesis, decision points, and practical materials.

Fractional strategic capacity

For organizations that need senior strategic support without building a permanent internal role around the need.
