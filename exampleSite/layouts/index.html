About

Independent strategic advisory grounded in healthcare, associations, digital health, and execution.

Galanou Consulting is led by Dr. Georgia Galanou Luchen, a Greek-American healthcare strategist with a pharmacy background and experience across professional associations, digital health, AI, education, and stakeholder-driven transformation.

Her work sits at the intersection of strategy, execution, and cross-functional alignment: helping organizations clarify complex work, align the right people, and turn emerging ideas into practical plans, materials, and next steps.
